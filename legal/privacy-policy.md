# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date: [EFFECTIVE DATE]**

This Privacy Policy explains how [LEGAL ENTITY NAME] ("BackNine," "we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and shares information when you use the BackNine application and related services (the "Service"). By using the Service, you agree to this Policy. If you do not agree, do not use the Service.

BackNine is intended only for individuals **18 years of age or older**. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone under 18.

## 1. A note about health information

BackNine handles information about your body and health (such as sleep, heart-rate variability, activity, weight, body composition, nutrition, mood, and lab results you enter). We treat this as **sensitive information** and handle it with care. Please note that BackNine is a consumer product and is **not** a HIPAA-covered entity or a healthcare provider; the data you store in BackNine is generally not "protected health information" under HIPAA. We protect it under this Policy and applicable consumer-privacy laws.

## 2. Information we collect

**a. Information you provide:**

- **Account & identity:** email address and/or third-party login identifier (e.g., Google), and a display name.
- **Health & fitness profile:** date of birth/age, sex, height, weight, body composition, fitness markers (e.g., VO2 max), and similar details you enter.
- **Logs you create:** workouts, weigh-ins, meals and nutrition (including meal photos you upload), mood and daily check-ins, goals, and lab/biomarker information you choose to enter.
- **Social content:** friend connections, groups/crews you join, messages (direct and group), reactions, comments, gear reviews, and participation in challenges and leaderboards.
- **Coach Al inputs:** the text, descriptions, and images you submit to our AI features.
- **Communications:** messages you send us (e.g., support requests).

**b. Information from connected services:**

- **Oura Ring:** with your authorization, readiness, sleep, heart-rate variability, body temperature, activity, steps, and related metrics.
- **Apple Health:** data you choose to export to us (e.g., via Health Auto Export), such as steps, sleep, and heart-rate variability.
- **Other connected devices** you choose to link.

**c. Information collected automatically:**

- **Device & usage data:** such as IP address, device/browser type, app interactions, and log data, used to operate and secure the Service.
- **Local storage:** we store an authentication token and limited preferences on your device, and use a service worker to enable installation and offline functionality. We do not use third-party advertising cookies.

## 3. How we use information

We use information to:

- provide, operate, personalize, and improve the Service (including dashboards, scores, trends, history, and social features);
- generate AI features ("Coach Al") such as insights, briefings, goal plans, and meal/workout parsing;
- enable the features you choose to use, including sharing activity with friends and groups you connect with;
- understand demand (for example, aggregated, de-identified analysis of what people search for in the gear store) to improve our catalog;
- communicate with you about the Service and respond to your requests;
- maintain security, prevent fraud and abuse, and comply with legal obligations.

We do **not** sell your personal information, and we do **not** use your health data for third-party advertising.

## 4. AI processing

To provide Coach Al features, certain inputs and health data are sent to our third-party AI provider (currently **Anthropic**, which provides the Claude models) for processing. This may include chat messages, workout/meal descriptions, meal photos, and relevant health metrics needed to generate a response. This data is processed to return results to you and is handled under the provider's terms. AI output may be inaccurate and is not professional advice (see the [Medical & Health Disclaimer](./medical-disclaimer.md)).

## 5. How we share information

We share information only as described here:

- **Service providers / sub-processors** that help us run the Service, under contractual confidentiality obligations, including:
  - **Supabase** – database and authentication
  - **Vercel** – application hosting (front end)
  - **Render** – application hosting (back end)
  - **Anthropic** – AI processing (Coach Al)
  - **Oura** – wearable data (with your authorization)
  - **Apple Health / export tools** – data you choose to send us
- **Other users**, when you use social features: information such as your display name, activity milestones, scores, streaks, leaderboard standings, group/challenge participation, gear reviews, comments, and messages is shared with the friends or groups you connect with.
- **Legal and safety**: when we believe disclosure is required by law or legal process, or is necessary to protect the rights, property, or safety of BackNine, our users, or the public.
- **Business transfers**: in connection with a merger, acquisition, financing, or sale of assets, subject to this Policy.
